Duncan Geere

I'm an award-winning writer, editor and data journalist with more than 10 years of experience. I cover the intersection of science, technology and culture, with a particular interest in environmental issues.

I'm currently working as a senior creative producer at Information is Beautiful, and I've contributed freelance work to publications like Wired, BBC Wildlife, BBC Science Focus, Technology Review, Popular Science, NME, PC Gamer, Techradar and Men's Health.

All my business operations are 100% carbon-negative. I reduce my emissions annually, and offset the remaining sum with Gold Standard offsets.

Originally from the UK, now based in Sweden.

Soviet Space Dogs Database

Cataloguing the heroism of the dogs who participated in the Soviet space program in the 1950s and 1960s.

I put together a database of space dogs and missions that they flew on based on the lists in the back of Olesya Turkina’s book “Soviet Space Dogs”.

I’m working on a few visualisations of the data, but if you create and publish something based on the database then please email and let me know about it.
